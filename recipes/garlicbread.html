Garlic Bread

Make this classic side dish with a crispy crust and buttery, garlicky centre

Our cheesy garlic bread toasts are delicious served with lasagne, soups and plenty more.

Ingredients

  • 2 ciabatta loaves
  • 140g butter, softened
  • 4-6 garlic cloves, crushed
  • handful parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp finely grated Parmesan (or vegetarian alternative)

Steps

  1. Slice the bread in half lengthwise.
  2. Toast the crust side for 2 mins until really crispy
  3. Mix together butter, garlic and parsley in a bowl and spread the mix over the cut bread halves
  4. Sprinkle over the Parmesan
  5. Just before serving place on a baking sheet, then cook under a hot grill for 5 mins until toasty and lightly golden. Let it cool for 1 min before cutting into thick slices.
