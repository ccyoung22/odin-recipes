Aperol Spritz

Aperol spritzes are ultra refreshing, not-too-sweet bubbly cocktails.

A proper Aperol spritz tastes like summertime in Italy and looks like a golden orange sunset. Iâ€™ll gladly sip one on our front porch now, overlooking my front garden, or preferably, with a view of the Italian Riviera.

Ingredients

  • 100ml Aperol
  • 150ml Prosecco
  • Soda Water
  • 2 slices of fresh orange

Steps

  1. Put a couple of cubes of ice into 2 glasses
  2. Add a 50 ml measure of Aperol to each glass
  3. Divide the prosecco between the glasses and then top up with soda to taste
  4. Finally, add a slice of fresh orange to each glass and serve.
